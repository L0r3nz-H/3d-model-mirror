Looking for a simple yet effective solution to securely attach your Apple AirTag to various objects? Look no further! This 3D printable holder is designed specifically for this purpose, featuring convenient ZipTie mounting points and drain holes for added versatility.

The ZipTie holes are sized at 2x5mm, ensuring compatibility with standard ZipTies for hassle-free attachment.

Designed with ease of use in mind, this model requires no supports and should be printed directly on the bottom surface. It's optimized for 0.2mm layer height, 0.4mm nozzle, and PLA material, but it's adaptable to smaller dimensions and other materials as well.
